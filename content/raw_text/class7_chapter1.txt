Question 1:
Word: महोत्सवः
IAST: mahotsavaḥ
Rule: Ād Guṇaḥ (ā + u becomes o)
Sutra: 6.1.87
Attribution: Panini Ashtadhyayi 6.1.87 / NCERT Class 7 Chapter 4
Options:
- Mahā + Utsavaḥ (Guṇa) [CORRECT]
- Maho + Tsavaḥ (Dīrgha)
- Mahā + Tsavaḥ (Vṛddhi)
- Maha + Utsavaḥ (Yan)
Hint: The ending vowel 'ā' in 'Mahā' meets the starting vowel 'u' in 'Utsavaḥ' to form the Guṇa vowel 'o'.
