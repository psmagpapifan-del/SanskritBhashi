Grammar Appendices (परिशिष्‍टम्)

  - Varnamatra-Parichayah (Phonetics):
      - Hrasvah (ह्रस्वः): Short vowel (1 beat).
      - Dirghah (दीर्घः): Long vowel (2 beats).
      - Plutah (प्लुतः): Extra-long vowel (3 beats).
      - Ardhamatrikam (अर्धमात्रिकम्): Consonants (half beat).
  - Shabdarupani (Noun Declensions): Covers 'a'-ending masculine (Deva, Rama),
    'i'-ending (Kavi, Hari), 'u'-ending (Shambhu, Guru), and Feminine 'a'-ending
    (Mala, Rama).
  - Dhatarupani (Verb Conjugations): Covers Present (Lat), Future (Lrit), and
    Past (Lang) tenses for roots like Path (to read), Likha (to write), As (to
    be), and Kri (to do).
